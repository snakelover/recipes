                     Macadamia Nut Rice

Recipe By     : 
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Side Dish                        
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  chopped macadamia nuts
     1/4      teaspoon  (1/4 to 1/2) crushed hot red pepper flakes
       2     teaspoons  butter or margarine
       3          cups  cooked rice
       2   tablespoons  grated Parmesan cheese
   1 1/2   tablespoons  chopped fresh parsley

Cook nuts with pepper flakes in butter in large skillet over medium-high heat
until nuts are lightly browned.

Add rice, cheese and parsley; stir until thoroughly heated.
